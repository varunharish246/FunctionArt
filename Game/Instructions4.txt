1.You will have thirty seconds to graph one function, you can use the JMenuBars to change the 
equation, and use the JMenuBar on the right to change the domains.
2. When you press submit the equation will be checked, if you get it right a green message 
will appear telling you to keep on going, if you get it wrong a red message will appear telling
you to try again
3. After you finish a level a green message will appear and congratulate you, then you will be taken to the next level.
When you finish the game a green message will congratulate you for beating the game and ask you to quit to go
to the Home Page.
