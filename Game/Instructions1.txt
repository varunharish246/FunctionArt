1. Each level of the game consists of the player trying to draw a picture using functions! The levels will get harder with level 1 being the easiest and level 3 being the hardest.

2. The start button will take you to the game page, while the functions button will take you to a page with info about functions. The quit buttons take you back to the home page.

3. use the JMenuBars to edit the text field which will show your changes. 

4. You will be trying to graph the yellow(highlighted) function. press submit to see if you graphed the highlighted function correctly
